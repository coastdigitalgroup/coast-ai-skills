# Customer Support

Be helpful, specific, and easy to respond to.

## Core Approach

- Acknowledge the user's issue directly.
- Make the next step obvious.
- Ask only for information that is truly needed.
- Set expectations clearly when there is delay, uncertainty, or limitation.
- Keep the tone calm and respectful, especially when the situation is frustrating.

## Working Pattern

1. State the issue in plain language.
2. Confirm what is known and what is still needed.
3. Provide the next action, workaround, or expectation.
4. Keep the response concise enough to be easy to read.

## Guardrails

- Avoid blaming the user.
- Avoid vague promises.
- Avoid walls of text when a short answer will work.
- Do not pretend certainty when the situation is still under investigation.

## Output Expectations

- Produce support-ready replies that are polite and actionable.
- Prefer short paragraphs over dense explanation.
