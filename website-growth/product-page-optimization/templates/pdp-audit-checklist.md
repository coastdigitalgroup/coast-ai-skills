# Product Detail Page (PDP) Friction Audit

Use this checklist to identify bottlenecks on an e-commerce product page. A "No"
to any of these indicates a potential conversion leak.

## 1. Visuals & Presentation

- [ ] **High Resolution:** Can the user zoom in to see texture and detail?
- [ ] **Context:** Is there at least one "lifestyle" image showing the product
      in use?
- [ ] **Completeness:** Are all angles of the product shown (front, back, side,
      bottom)?
- [ ] **Video/Motion:** Is there a video or GIF showing the product's function
      or movement?

## 2. The Buy-Box (ATC Area)

- [ ] **Contrast:** Is the Add-to-Cart (ATC) button a high-contrast color that
      stands out?
- [ ] **Mobile Visibility:** Is the ATC button visible in the first view on
      mobile without scrolling?
- [ ] **Price Clarity:** Is the price (including any discounts) clearly
      displayed within 50px of the ATC button?
- [ ] **Variant Friction:** If there are colors/sizes, are they selectable via
      swatches/buttons rather than a hidden dropdown?
- [ ] **Default Selection:** Is a popular variant selected by default so the
      user can click ATC immediately?

## 3. Shipping & Risk Reversal

- [ ] **Cost Transparency:** Is shipping cost (or "Free Shipping") mentioned
      near the price?
- [ ] **Delivery Dates:** Does the page show an estimated delivery date (e.g.,
      "Get it by Tuesday")?
- [ ] **Guarantees:** Is there a "30-Day Returns" or warranty badge near the ATC
      button?
- [ ] **Availability:** Is stock status clear (e.g., "In Stock" or "Only 2
      Left")?

## 4. Product Story & Copy

- [ ] **Benefit Headers:** Do subheaders describe a benefit (e.g., "Sleep
      Cooler") rather than a feature (e.g., "Breathable Fabric")?
- [ ] **Scannability:** Is the description broken into bullet points or short
      paragraphs?
- [ ] **Objection Handling:** Does the copy answer the #1 reason why someone
      might hesitate (e.g., "Dishwasher Safe," "Fits All Sizes")?

## 5. Social Proof

- [ ] **Star Rating:** Is the average star rating visible near the product
      title?
- [ ] **Review Count:** Is the total number of reviews displayed?
- [ ] **Authenticity:** Are there photos from real customers in the review
      section?

---

### Audit Scoring

- **0-3 "No"s:** Optimized page. Focus on minor A/B testing.
- **4-7 "No"s:** Moderate Friction. Prioritize ATC visibility and shipping
  transparency.
- **8+ "No"s:** Critical Failure. Significant revenue is being lost due to
  shoper anxiety.
