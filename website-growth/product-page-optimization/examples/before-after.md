# Before and After: E-commerce Product Page Optimization

## Scenario: Premium Ergonomic Desk Chair

**Context:** An online furniture retailer selling a $600 ergonomic chair. High
traffic from social ads, but the Add-to-Cart (ATC) rate is stuck at 1.8%.

---

### Before: The "Information Gap" PDP

- **Visuals:** 2 generic studio shots on a white background. No zoom.
- **Buy-Box:**
  - Price buried under a long "Overview" paragraph.
  - "Add to Cart" button is light grey and blends into the footer.
  - Size selection is a hidden dropdown that defaults to "Select Size."
- **Description:** A 500-word block of technical jargon (e.g., "features a
  dual-pivot synchronous tilt mechanism").
- **Trust:** No reviews visible above the fold; "Shipping calculated at
  checkout" message next to the price.

**Result:** Users are confused by the technical jargon and anxious about the
high price + unknown shipping costs.

---

### After: The "Clarity & Confidence" PDP

- **Visuals:**
  - 6 high-res angles with "Hover to Zoom."
  - 1 "Lifestyle" video showing the chair in a modern home office.
  - 1 "Dimensions" graphic showing height/width adjustments.
- **Buy-Box (Optimized Hierarchy):**
  - **H1:** "The ErgoFlow 360 – All-Day Lumbar Support"
  - **Price:** Bolded $599.00 with "Free Express Shipping" in green right below
    it.
  - **Variants:** Large "Clickable Swatches" for colors (no dropdowns).
  - **CTA:** Bright orange "Add to Cart - Ships Tomorrow" button.
- **Description (Scannable Benefits):**
  - **Header:** "Never Feel Stiff Again."
  - **Bullet Points:** "3D Armrests: Adjusts to your desk height to prevent
    shoulder strain."
  - **FAQ Section:** Addresses "Will it fit my height?" (range 5'2" to 6'4").
- **Trust:**
  - "4.8/5 Stars (1,240 Reviews)" linked right under the Title.
  - "30-Day Risk-Free Trial" badge next to the ATC button.

**Result:** ATC rate increased from 1.8% to 3.4%. Return rate dropped by 12%
because users understood the dimensions before buying.
