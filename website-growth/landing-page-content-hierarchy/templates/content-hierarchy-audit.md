# Landing Page Content Hierarchy Audit Checklist

Use this checklist to evaluate the logical flow and persuasive structure of any
landing page.

## Phase 1: The "10-Second Story" Test

- [ ] Can I understand the primary value proposition by reading **only the H1
      and H2s**?
- [ ] Do the headlines describe **benefits** (outcomes) rather than just
      **features** (functions)?
- [ ] Is there a clear logical progression between the first three sections?

## Phase 2: Structural Integrity

- [ ] **Context Before Action:** Is the problem or value established before the
      first major CTA?
- [ ] **Evidence Proximity:** Is there social proof or data within one scroll of
      the primary claim?
- [ ] **The "How it Works" Section:** Does it appear _after_ the "What it is"
      and _before_ the pricing/final CTA?
- [ ] **Objection Handling:** Are common hesitations (price, security,
      integration) addressed on the page?
- [ ] **The Narrative Thread:** If I moved Section X to the top, would the page
      still make sense? (If yes, the narrative is too weak).

## Phase 3: Scannability & Visual Hierarchy

- [ ] **Section Contrast:** Does each section have a distinct visual boundary
      (color shift, padding, or divider)?
- [ ] **One Idea Rule:** Does each content block focus on exactly ONE key
      message?
- [ ] **Scanning Patterns:** Are the most important keywords placed on the left
      (F-pattern) or along the diagonal (Z-pattern)?
- [ ] **Button Prominence:** Is the CTA the most visually "heavy" element in its
      section?

## Phase 4: Friction & Cognitive Load

- [ ] **Repetition Check:** Have I removed any sections that repeat information
      unnecessarily?
- [ ] **Feature Prioritization:** Are the top 3 most important features
      highlighted, with the rest secondary?
- [ ] **Visual Overload:** Are there long paragraphs of text (more than 3 lines)
      that should be broken into bullets or icons?

## Summary Score

- **0-5 Checks:** High Friction. The page likely feels like a wall of text or a
  random collection of parts.
- **6-10 Checks:** Moderate Clarity. The core message is there, but the flow is
  disjointed.
- **11-15 Checks:** High Conversion Potential. The page tells a logical story
  and guides the user toward action.
