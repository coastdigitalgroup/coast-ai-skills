## 3. Optimization Checklist
Apply these rules to the new hero design.

### Copywriting
- [ ] **H1 Headline:** Focuses on a result or benefit (Clear over Clever).
- [ ] **Subheadline:** Explains *how* the benefit is achieved or for *whom*.
- [ ] **CTA Text:** Action-oriented and benefit-driven (e.g., "Get My Report").
- [ ] **Microcopy:** Added trust signals near CTA (e.g., "No CC required").

### Design & Layout
- [ ] **Button Contrast:** The CTA color stands out from the background.
- [ ] **"Above the Fold":** All critical elements (H1, Sub, CTA) are visible without scrolling.
- [ ] **Negative Space:** Enough white space to make the text and CTA readable.
- [ ] **Visual Direction:** Images or icons point/draw attention toward the CTA.

### Validation
- [ ] **Mobile Check:** Tested the layout on a small screen.
- [ ] **Contrast Check:** Text is readable against the background image/color.
- [ ] **5-Second Test:** Ready to show to a neutral party.
