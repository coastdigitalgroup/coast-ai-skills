# Pricing Page Optimization Audit Checklist

Use this checklist to evaluate and improve the effectiveness of any pricing
page.

## 1. Choice Architecture & Clarity

- [ ] **Is there a "Most Popular" or "Recommended" plan?** (Provides a shortcut
      for the user).
- [ ] **Are there 4 or fewer plans?** (Avoids choice paralysis).
- [ ] **Is the difference between tiers immediately obvious?** (e.g., "10 users
      vs. Unlimited").
- [ ] **Are plan names descriptive of the user persona?** (e.g., "Freelancer,"
      "Business," "Enterprise").

## 2. Information Hierarchy

- [ ] **Are only the top 3-5 value-drivers listed in the cards?** (Keeps it
      scannable).
- [ ] **Is there a detailed comparison table below the fold?** (For users who
      need technical details).
- [ ] **Is the primary CTA for the target plan visually distinct?** (Higher
      contrast).
- [ ] **Is the price font size the most prominent numerical element?**

## 3. Copy & Psychological Triggers

- [ ] **Are CTAs benefit-driven?** (e.g., "Get Started" vs. "Select").
- [ ] **Is the "Billed Annually" vs. "Monthly" toggle clear?**
- [ ] **Is the highest-value tier placed strategically?** (Usually the middle or
      first option).
- [ ] **Are there clear risk reversal signals?** (e.g., "30-day guarantee," "No
      credit card required").

## 4. Trust & Social Proof

- [ ] **Are there trust badges or logos near the CTAs?**
- [ ] **Is there at least one testimonial relevant to the value of the
      product?**
- [ ] **Is there a brief FAQ section addressing price/billing concerns?**

## 5. Technical & UX

- [ ] **Does the page load in under 2 seconds?** (Pricing is high-intent, don't
      lose them to lag).
- [ ] **Are the cards mobile-responsive?** (Do they stack correctly?).
- [ ] **Is the currency correct for the user's region?**
- [ ] **Is there a clear way to contact sales for custom needs?**

---

## Plan Structure Template (The "Golden Ratio")

If you are redesigning from scratch, follow this "Standard High-Convert"
structure:

1.  **Headline:** Focus on the value of the solution.
2.  **Toggle:** Monthly vs. Annual (default to Annual).
3.  **Pricing Cards (3):**
    - **Left:** Entry-level (Anchoring).
    - **Center:** Target/Pro (Highlighted, Most Popular).
    - **Right:** High-tier (Premium features).
4.  **Risk Reversal:** Underneath the CTAs.
5.  **Trust Bar:** Logo garden of customers.
6.  **Detailed Comparison Table:** Feature-by-feature breakdown.
7.  **FAQs:** 3-5 billing/plan questions.
8.  **Secondary CTA:** "Need something custom? Contact Sales."
