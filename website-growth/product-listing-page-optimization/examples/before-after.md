# Product Listing Page: Before & After

## Scenario: Consumer Electronics Retailer (Headphones Category)

The retailer has high traffic to their "Over-Ear Headphones" category page, but
the Click-Through Rate (CTR) to individual product pages is low (under 3%).
Users are frequently clicking back to the category page within seconds
(Pogo-sticking).

### Before: The "Information Gap" PLP

**Characteristics:**

- **Filters:** 15 different filter categories (Brand, Color, Price, Weight,
  Driver Size, Battery Life, etc.) all expanded on the left sidebar.
- **Product Cards:**
  - Small thumbnail image.
  - Product title only (e.g., "WH-1000XM5").
  - No price visible (requires click).
  - No star ratings.
- **Mobile:** Filters are at the very bottom of the long list of products.
- **Result:** Users have to click into every product just to see the price and
  if it's "top-rated," leading to frustration and high bounce rates.

---

### After: The "Discovery Hub" PLP

**Optimizations Applied:**

1.  **Faceted Search:**
    - **3-Filter Rule:** Only "Price," "Brand," and "Noise Canceling" are
      expanded by default.
    - **Selection Pills:** Active filters appear as removable pills at the top
      of the grid.
2.  **Product Card (Three-Point Rule):**
    - **Anchor:** Large, high-res lifestyle image showing the headphones being
      worn.
    - **Context:**
      - Title: "Sony WH-1000XM5 Noise Canceling Headphones" (Descriptive).
      - Price: "$348.00" (Bold and clear).
      - Proof: "4.8/5 Stars (12,400 reviews)".
      - Tech Spec: "30-Hour Battery Life" (Key decision factor for this
        category).
    - **Target:** "View Details" button + "Quick Add" for repeat buyers.
3.  **Mobile discovery:**
    - **Filter Drawer:** A "Filter & Sort" floating button stays sticky at the
      bottom of the viewport.
    - **Result Count:** The "Apply" button inside the drawer says "Show 42
      Results."

### Measured Outcome:

- **PDP CTR:** Increased from 2.8% to 6.5%.
- **Pogo-Sticking:** Reduced by 40% because users knew the price and key specs
  before clicking.
- **Conversion Rate:** 12% lift in category-originated sales.
