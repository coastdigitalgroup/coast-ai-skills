# PLP Optimization Audit Checklist

Use this checklist to evaluate and optimize any Product Listing Page or Category
Page.

## 1. Visual & Information Hierarchy (The Product Card)

- [ ] **The 5-Second Test:** Can a user identify the product, price, and key
      differentiator (e.g., color, size, spec) within 5 seconds without
      clicking?
- [ ] **Visual Scent:** Does the card indicate variations (e.g., "Available in 5
      colors")?
- [ ] **Social Proof:** Is the star rating visible for items with >5 reviews?
- [ ] **Clarity:** Is the price the second most prominent element after the
      product image?
- [ ] **Primary Action:** Is there a clear path to the PDP (or "Quick Add" for
      low-complexity items)?

## 2. Faceted Search & Navigation

- [ ] **The 3-Filter Rule:** Are only the top 3 most relevant filter categories
      expanded by default on desktop?
- [ ] **Dynamic Counts:** Do filter options show the number of results in
      parentheses?
- [ ] **Zero-State Handling:** Are filters with zero results hidden or disabled
      to prevent "No Results Found" errors?
- [ ] **Visual Filters:** Are visual attributes (colors, patterns) represented
      with swatches instead of text?
- [ ] **Active States:** Are selected filters clearly visible (e.g., in a
      "Applied Filters" bar) with an easy "Clear All" option?

## 3. Mobile Optimization

- [ ] **Grid Efficiency:** Is a 2-column grid used (unless products are highly
      detailed/technical)?
- [ ] **Filter Accessibility:** Is the filter trigger a persistent, easy-to-tap
      button (e.g., bottom-sticky or prominent header)?
- [ ] **Interaction Pattern:** Do filters open in a full-screen drawer or modal
      rather than pushing content down?
- [ ] **Touch Targets:** Are all filter checkboxes and sort options at least
      44x44px?

## 4. Sorting & Discovery

- [ ] **Conversion-Led Defaults:** Is the default sort order "Best Sellers" or
      "Recommended" rather than "Price: Low to High" or "Newest"?
- [ ] **Badge Strategy:** Are "Sale," "New," or "Best Seller" badges used
      sparingly to highlight high-value items?
- [ ] **Loading Performance:** Does the page use "Load More" or "Infinite
      Scroll" with a way to reach the footer (or traditional pagination for
      large catalogs)?

## 5. Analytics & Health Checks

- [ ] **Pogo-Sticking:** Is the rate of <5s returns from PDP to PLP below 15%?
- [ ] **Filter Engagement:** Are at least 20% of users interacting with the
      faceted search?
- [ ] **Empty States:** Does searching for a non-existent combination of filters
      provide alternative suggestions?
