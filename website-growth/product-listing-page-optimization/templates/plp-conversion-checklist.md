# PLP Conversion Checklist

Use this checklist to audit any e-commerce category or search results page.

## 1. Visual Hierarchy & Scannability

- [ ] **Price Visibility:** Is the price (and any discount) clearly visible
      without hovering?
- [ ] **Title Clarity:** Are product titles concise and descriptive?
- [ ] **Image Consistency:** Do all product images use a consistent aspect ratio
      and background?
- [ ] **Badge Restraint:** Are there fewer than 2 badges per product card (e.g.,
      "New" or "Sale")?
- [ ] **Grid Balance:** Is the number of columns appropriate for the screen size
      (4 on desktop, 2 on mobile)?

## 2. Filtering & Discovery

- [ ] **Top 3 Facets:** Are the 3 most important filters (e.g., Size, Color,
      Price) expanded by default on desktop?
- [ ] **Filter Relevance:** Are filters specific to the category (e.g., "Thread
      Count" for sheets, "Resolution" for TVs)?
- [ ] **Selection Persistence:** Do filters remain selected if the user
      navigates back from a PDP?
- [ ] **Zero Results:** Does a "No results" state provide a "Clear All Filters"
      button?
- [ ] **Count Visibility:** Does each filter option show the number of products
      available (e.g., "Blue (12)")?

## 3. Product Card Interactions

- [ ] **Quick Add:** Can standardized products (one-size or simple variants) be
      added directly to the cart?
- [ ] **Secondary Imagery:** Does hovering over the card show an alternate view
      (e.g., lifestyle shot or back of garment)?
- [ ] **Social Proof:** Is a star rating and review count visible for every
      product?
- [ ] **Variant Indicators:** Are color swatches or size availability visible?

## 4. Mobile Experience

- [ ] **Sticky Discovery:** Are the "Filter" and "Sort" buttons sticky or easily
      accessible at the top?
- [ ] **Touch Targets:** Are all clickable elements (swatches, buttons) at least
      44px?
- [ ] **Loading Pattern:** Does the page use "Load More" or Infinite Scroll with
      a footer rather than pagination?
- [ ] **Filter UI:** Is the mobile filter interface a full-screen "drawer" that
      is easy to close?
