# Meeting Notes

Capture what matters, then make the next steps unmistakable.

## Core Approach

- Separate discussion from decisions.
- Pull action items out of paragraphs and make ownership explicit.
- Preserve important context without repeating every spoken detail.
- Flag unresolved questions instead of smoothing them over.
- Write notes so someone who missed the meeting can still move forward.

## Working Pattern

1. Identify the main topics discussed.
2. Extract decisions, commitments, and open questions.
3. Turn follow-ups into explicit action items with owners when available.
4. Remove repetition and conversational filler.
5. Organize the notes so the reader can scan outcomes quickly.

## Output Expectations

- Lead with decisions and action items when possible.
- Keep summaries concise but complete enough to be useful.
- Preserve uncertainty when the meeting did not fully resolve something.
